The benefits of working with us

Open and close your property when you want
We give you the freedom to open or close your property on our site whenever you want. With no lock-in contracts, you're in control of your property.
What happens when property is damaged?
Property owners can request damage deposits from guests.Deposits hhelp cover any potential damage caused by a guest,offering reassurance that your property will be treated respectfully.
How do I benefit from Pallazo.com
We hold regular meetings with our partners to discuss how to build a relationship around their needs and how to grow the business.We also connect you directly to the clients.
Use Pallazo.com alongside other platforms
Using Pallazo.com along with your other relationships isn't a problem! With our simple calendar, you can manage your availability parallel to your existing relationships.
